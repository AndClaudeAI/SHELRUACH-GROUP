Welcome to Charterhouse Lagos, the first British independent school in West Africa and a member of the Charterhouse family of schools located in the heart of Nigeria’s economic capital, Lagos. We are an academically selective school offering the British curriculum.

Find out more about our state-of-the-art facilities, 400 years of heritage, and world-class teachers.

We offer boarding and day options for primary and secondary school.

Admissions are now open — begin your child’s journey today

World-class teachers: evidence-based learning

At Charterhouse Lagos, we deliver a forward-thinking education built on the British curriculum and evidence-based teaching. We empower skilled teachers with proven training methods to create a dynamic, respectful, and academically-focused environment where children excel.
