Welcome to Shelruach IMO, a community-centred school in the heart of Imo State, Nigeria, where every child is known, valued and inspired to reach their full potential.

We bring together dedicated teachers, a thoughtfully designed curriculum, and a warm, purposeful environment so that learning is joyful and rigorous in equal measure.

We offer day and boarding options across our primary and secondary schools.

Admissions are now open — begin your child’s journey today.

Dedicated teachers, learner-centred education

At Shelruach IMO we believe great teaching changes lives. Our staff combine strong subject knowledge with proven, evidence-informed methods to create classrooms that are dynamic, respectful and focused on every learner’s progress.

Applications are welcome throughout the year, with main intakes ahead of each new term.

Boarding places are available for our primary and secondary year groups, with weekly and full boarding options.

Our admissions team is happy to arrange a visit so you can experience the Shelruach community first-hand.

Get in touch today to learn more about the process and how to secure your child’s place.

Secondary School

Our secondary curriculum builds a solid foundation in core subjects while encouraging curiosity, independence and depth of understanding — preparing students for the next stage of their education and beyond.

With pastoral care and safeguarding at the heart of everything we do, and boarding that feels like a true home away from home, your child will be supported to thrive.
